How to Use

1

Select or Record a Video

Choose a video from your photo library, or record a new one directly in the app. Ideal for capturing swings, throws, jumps, or any repetitive motion.

2

Configure Settings

Adjust the frame interval to control extraction density. Optionally trim the video range, enable person segmentation to isolate the subject, choose background color, or flip the image horizontally.

3

Start Analysis

The app extracts frames at your chosen interval. If "Auto Skip Similar Frames" is on, near-duplicate frames are automatically removed, keeping only meaningful motion changes.

4

Review & Select Frames

Browse the extracted frames in a scrollable strip. Tap to select the key frames you want to keep. Use the grid overlay for alignment reference.

5

Export

Create a strobe photo (grid composite), export as MP4 video, or save individual frames. Share your results via the system share sheet or save directly to your Camera Roll.
